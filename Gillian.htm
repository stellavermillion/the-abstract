Title: Stairway to Success

Date Created: April 02, 2021

In our life, we don’t easily get what we want. In order to achieve a desired goal, we need to face challenges and hardships. We should know what should be prioritized first. In this artwork, it depicts our way to success, to achieve our goal, we need to take it step by step, and that we should take it easy. If we rush it, maybe we can get to our goal earlier, but we may also fall out of the stair.

 

Title: Self-Portrait

Date Created: April 02, 2021

While a portrait refers to any painting that depicts a human figure, a self-portrait refers to a painting or drawing that depicts the artist that produced it. Self-portraiture, whether produced in the medium of painting or of photography, is its own distinct genre of art, like that of the landscape or the still life. That is, the common features of all self-portraits unify the genre in content and means of expression.

Title: Bouquet of Love

Date Created: February 20, 2021

A lot of people use flowers to express their feelings. Men, for example, declare their undying love to the women of their dreams by sending flowers. Flowers have different meanings depending on their color. In this Bouquet of Love, the flowers are colored red and pink; red, which is the universal language of love, that also speaks of desire and passion, and pink which is a sign of happiness. According to the artist, she made it while thinking of her grandmother whom she cares deeply. She wants to show her grandmother how she loves her and she wants her to be happy at the same time.
